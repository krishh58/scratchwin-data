How It Works

Three numbers tell you everything you need to know about a scratch ticket.

1

EV % — Prize Pool Remaining

How much of the original prize money is still unclaimed. 100% = brand new game, 0% = all prizes gone. Shop fresh games, avoid picked-over ones.

2

Win % — Your Break-Even Odds

The estimated chance your ticket wins back at least what you paid — calculated from real remaining prize counts, not the printed odds on the back.

3

🔥 Fire Rating — The Final Score

We combine both metrics into a 1–5 rating ranked against every other game in your state. Pick 5-fire tickets. Skip 1-fire ones. Simple.
