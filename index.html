How It Works

Three numbers tell you everything you need to know about a scratch ticket.

1

EV % — Prize Pool Remaining

How much of the original prize money is still unclaimed. 100% = brand new game, 0% = all prizes gone. Shop fresh games, avoid picked-over ones.

2

Win % — Your Break-Even Odds

The estimated chance your ticket wins back at least what you paid. Calculated from real remaining prize data — not the printed odds on the back of the ticket.

3

🔥 Fire Rating — Combined Score

We combine both metrics into a 1–5 rating ranked against every other game in your state. Pick a 5-fire ticket. Skip the 1-fire ones.
